        We are growing our team! As an Analytics Consultancy part of what we do is also working to create Automation Tools and Products for our valued Global Customers.
        


        We would love to welcome a Product Management Consultant who can assist us for an initial 3-6 month period to work on a 'Go To Market' strategy for our growing product suite. 
        


        You will have the opportunity to join full time if this newly created position is one that develops into an awesome long term addition to our team. You will have some experience in marketing tech products previously ideally SAS or Martech related offerings and any prior exposure to Digital businesses and/or the analytics world would be a distinct advantage. 
        


        To highlight examples of ideal experience to bring to the table would be:
        


        At least 2-3 years experience in a relevant role

        Previous exposure to Product Marketing 

        Understanding the need to layout a Product Roadmap and align to a marketing plan and calendar 

        Ability to understand the technical features and benefits of the products being marketed and potential audience to market to

        Some degree of experience within Martech or the Technology Industry would be an advantage 

        Demonstrable knowledge of creating and delivering clear ' Go to Market' plans is a must for this role 

        A solid understanding of the commercial impact the Product Roadmap has to a Software as a Service business line with a Consultancy like Sparkline
        


        You will have great passion for what you do, a hunger to learn and be excited by the prospect and the opportunity to work within a 'Small but Mighty' company like ours!
